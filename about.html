About Us

Founded in 2010 and officially registered in 2019, 3rd Alliance Global Concept LTD is an independent leader in state-of-the-art abattoir services, agricultural consulting, and general services. With innovative methodologies, we prioritize hygienic meat production and agricultural advancement, supplying quality meat products domestically and internationally. We collaborate with industry leaders to enrich our offerings and uphold safety and best practices.
